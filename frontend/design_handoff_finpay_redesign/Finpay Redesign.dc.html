Finpay — Linear-inspired redesign

A darker, quieter, more production-grade system. Warm-neutral surfaces layered by elevation, hairline borders instead of drop-shadows, tight typographic hierarchy, and the Finpay green reserved as a functional accent (primary action, positive balance, active state) rather than filling large areas. Photos are dropped in favour of type, iconography and real data. Numbers set in a mono face with tabular figures. Each surface below shows 3 directions — reference any by its id in chat (e.g. 1b).
